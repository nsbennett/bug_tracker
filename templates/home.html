Nathan's Bug Tracker

In one portal: log an issue, learn how it's going, get notified when it's done.

How it works

1. Register, log in, and post your issue

In your very own account, you can submit a support ticket. Keep track of your support tickets in your own private account.

2. Communicate with support staff

Click on each support ticket record you've submitted to see replies by support staff. You can communicate with them by reply comments.

3. Find out when it's done

Support staff will see all tickets submitted, so they'll be able to work with all the information all users have submitted. Watch for the reply comment letting you know it's all taken care of!
